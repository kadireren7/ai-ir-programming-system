Platform

A single gate for every automation system you run.

Torqa sits above your tools — not inside them. Connect a source and we begin scanning every workflow, run, and version against deterministic rules you control.

01 / Inspect

Static analysis, by design.

Every workflow JSON is parsed against a deterministic ruleset — no probabilistic scoring, no opaque models. You see exactly which rule fired and why.

02 / Govern

Policies as gates.

Compose strict, default, or custom policies. Enforce them in CI, on schedule, or as a webhook gate before runs ever reach production.

03 / Approve

Human in the loop, when it matters.

Borderline cases route to your reviewers with full context — diff, finding, severity, history. Decisions become signed records, not Slack messages.

Architecture

Above your stack, not inside it.

Torqa observes and gates without touching execution paths. Drop in a webhook, leave your runtime alone.
